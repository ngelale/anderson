About

I work on nonlinear dynamical systems, focusing on stability, bifurcation, Koopman operator theory, and networked systems. My goal is to develop structure-preserving models for real-world complex systems.

Research Themes

Bistable Dynamical Systems

Modeling tipping points and stability transitions using cubic normal forms.

Koopman Operator Methods

Designing physically grounded observable dictionaries for nonlinear systems.

Network Dynamics

Analyzing stability in interconnected systems such as power grids and finance.
